🤖 The Game

A survival game like no other

Forget zombies and combat. In Blossom, you play as a robot on Mars — a silent, barren planet. Your goal: transform it into a living world.

Instead of food, you manage electrical energy. Every step, every machine, every tool consumes power. And every decision shapes the planet around you.

Exploration, construction, automation, progressive terraforming... and somewhere, a mystery: what happened to Earth?

🌍

Open World

Freely explore on foot, by rover, or through the air. Discover ruins and abandoned stations.

⚡

Energy System

No hunger. You manage your electrical energy and build charging systems to stay alive.

🚗

Mobile Bases

Build modular rovers. Your base travels with you anywhere on the planet.

🌿

Ecosystems

Create chains of life: algae, insects, animals. Become the creator of a whole new world.

🪐 Gameplay

Terraforming, step by step

Every action transforms Mars. You start from a red desert and build, step by step, a living planet.

01

🌡️Raise the Pressure

Install atmospheric generators to thicken Mars's atmosphere. The very first step of any planetary transformation.

02

🔥Heat the Planet

Deploy solar reflectors and thermal power plants. Ice begins to melt. Water appears for the first time.

03

🪨Transform the Dust

Enrich the martian soil with bacteria and nutrients. Sterile rock slowly becomes fertile ground.

04

🌊Generate Oxygen

Plant the first algae in water pools. They breathe. The atmosphere truly begins to change.

05

🌳Grow Life

Trees, plants, flowers... The red planet turns green. A visually unique and deeply satisfying sight.

06

🦋Build Ecosystems

Insects, animals, natural cycles. Evolution takes hold. You're no longer a robot — you're a god of life.
